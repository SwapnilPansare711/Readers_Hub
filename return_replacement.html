Return & Replacement

At Readers Hub, we strive to provide high-quality books and a smooth shopping experience. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

Return

  • You can request a return within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • The book must be unused, in original condition, and with all packaging intact.
  • Returns will not be accepted for:
    • Used or damaged books (not caused during delivery)
    • Digital products or e-books

Replacement

  • Replacement is available if:
    • You received a damaged book
    • You received the wrong item
  • To request a replacement, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product.

Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Return Shipping

  • If the return is due to our mistake (wrong or damaged item), we will cover the shipping cost.
  • Otherwise, return shipping charges may apply.

How to Request

To initiate a return or replacement, contact us at:

  • Email: support@readershub.com
  • Phone: +91-XXXXXXXXXX

Please include:

  • Order ID
  • Reason for return/replacement
  • Photos (if applicable)
